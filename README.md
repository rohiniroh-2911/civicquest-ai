# CivicQuest AI

### Making Community Problem Reporting Smarter with AI

## Problem Statement

**Community Hero – Hyperlocal Problem Solver**


# About the Project

CivicQuest AI is an AI-powered platform that helps citizens report, track, verify, prioritize, and resolve local community issues more effectively.

People frequently encounter problems such as:

* Potholes
* Water leakages
* Overflowing garbage bins
* Broken streetlights
* Drainage issues
* Damaged public infrastructure

While many cities provide complaint portals, these systems are often slow, difficult to use, and lack transparency.

CivicQuest AI bridges this gap by combining Artificial Intelligence, community participation, gamification, and automation to make civic issue management efficient, transparent, and impactful.


# The Problem

Current civic issue reporting systems face several challenges:

* Duplicate complaints for the same issue
* No clear prioritization of urgent problems
* Lack of transparency in the resolution process
* Difficulty identifying the responsible department
* Limited community involvement after reporting
* Slow response times and poor follow-up mechanisms

As a result, many issues remain unresolved, affecting public safety, infrastructure quality, and citizen trust.


# Our Solution

CivicQuest AI enables citizens to upload a photo and description of a community problem.

The platform uses Google Gemini AI to:

* Analyze the issue
* Categorize the problem
* Assess severity
* Recommend the responsible authority
* Detect duplicate complaints
* Generate structured reports

Community members can verify reported issues while a public dashboard provides transparency into issue status and community participation.

# Project Workflow

## 1. Issue Reporting

Citizens upload:

* Issue Image
* Description
* Location

## 2. AI Analysis

Google Gemini AI analyzes the image and description to:

* Detect issue type
* Classify category
* Understand context
* Assess severity

## 3. Duplicate Detection

The system identifies similar nearby reports and groups duplicates together.

## 4. Authority Routing

The platform recommends the most appropriate department responsible for resolving the issue.

## 5. Community Verification

Citizens can:

* Confirm the issue exists
* Mark it as resolved
* Flag incorrect reports

## 6. Smart Prioritization

Issues are prioritized using:

* Severity Level
* Number of Reports
* Community Verification Count
* Time Since Reporting

## 7. Dashboard Visualization

All reported issues are displayed on a public dashboard.

## 8. Gamification & Rewards

Users earn:

* Points
* Badges
* Coupons
* Community Ranks

## 9. Predictive Insights

The platform identifies recurring civic problems and future hotspots.

## 10. Resolution & Closure

Authorities update issue status and resolved issues are marked as closed.

# Community Rewards Program

To encourage active citizen participation, CivicQuest AI introduces a reward-based engagement model.

Users earn points for:

* Reporting Genuine Issues
* Verifying Reports
* Confirming Resolutions
* Supporting Community Improvements

## Level System

| Level              | Requirement          | Reward                     |
| ------------------ | -------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Community Reporter | 10 Verified Reports  | 5% Coupon                  |
| Civic Contributor  | 25 Verified Reports  | 10% Coupon                 |
| Community Guardian | 50 Verified Reports  | 15% Coupon                 |
| City Champion      | 100 Verified Reports | 20% Coupon                 |
| Civic Hero         | 250 Verified Reports | 30% Coupon + Premium Badge |

## Reward Validation

Rewards are granted only when:

* Reports are Genuine
* Community Verification is Successful
* Issues are Resolved
* Duplicate Reports are Excluded

This prevents misuse of the system.
